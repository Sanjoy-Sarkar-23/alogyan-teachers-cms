# Notes Library

**References:** Google Drive (Grid view), Dropbox (File browsing).

## 1. Layout
A File Explorer style workspace. Sidebar for Batches/Folders ("Class 10", "Class 9"), main area showing a visual Grid of Materials.

## 2. Components
- **Folder Navigation:** Left alignment.
- **Material Card:** Rectangular card showing a large filetype icon (PDF, IMG, DOC), Title, and upload date. Hovering reveals actions (Download, Delete).
- **Search Bar:** Specifically filters the grid dynamically.

## 3. Tables
A toggle allows the Grid view to become a standard List View/Table (Filename, Format, Size, Batch, Uploaded By).

## 4. Forms
Top right Filter Dropdown to sort by "Newest" or "Oldest".

## 5. Buttons
Global Action: "+ Upload Notes" (Primary Red).

## 6. UX Flow
Teacher wants to reuse last year's notes -> Opens Notes -> Selects "Class 9 Science" folder -> Copies the link to the "Gravity Chapter" PDF -> Shares to new batch.

## 7. Mobile Layout
Grid transforms to a 2-column or 1-column scrollable list. Large thumbnails help visual recognition.

## 8. Empty States
"You haven't uploaded any study materials. Share a PDF or document with your students here."

## 9. Success States
"File downloaded to your device."

## 10. Error States
"Could not load notes library. Please check your connection."
