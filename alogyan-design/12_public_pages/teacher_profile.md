# Teacher Profile

**References:** Patreon (creator pages), Shopify (storefront simplicity).

## 1. Layout
A single-column landing page built for high conversion. Hero banner at top, Tabbed context (About, Reviews, Batches) below.

## 2. Components
- **Trust Badges:** "Verified Teacher", "⭐ 4.9/5 Average".
- **Gallery Grid:** Embedded Instagram feed or simple 3x3 photo grid.
- **Batch Cards:** "Class 10 Weekend", showing seats remaining and pricing.

## 3. Tables
N/A - Data is visualized hierarchically instead of tabular.

## 4. Forms
The only form is the lead gen modal ("Enquire for Trial Class") connected to a "Hook/CTA" button pinned to the screen bottom.

## 5. Buttons
Primary Conversion Tool: "Enquire Now" floating CTA.

## 6. UX Flow
Visits URL -> Scans rating and video intro -> Clicks Enquire -> Fills form -> Gets paired.

## 7. Mobile Layout
Heavy mobile optimization. Important CTAs become fixed to the viewport bottom area `(bottom-0)` safely above iOS/Android home swipers.

## 8. Empty States
Sections with zero content (e.g., zero reviews) simply do not render.

## 9. Success States
"We've sent your request to [Teacher Name]!"

## 10. Error States
"Phone verification failed. Please try a different number."
